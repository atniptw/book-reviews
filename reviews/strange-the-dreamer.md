# Strange the Dreamer

**Author:** Laini Taylor

**Rating:** 4/5

**Review Date:** 2026-02-13

---

## Review

This book was enjoyable and fun. The story delivers an engaging experience with compelling characters that draw you in.

## What I Liked

- Excellent storytelling that keeps you engaged throughout
- Fun and memorable characters

## What I Disliked

- The book had more of a romance novel feel than expected, which wasn't what I was anticipating going into it
