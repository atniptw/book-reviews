# Oh! My useless Goddess!

**Author:** Natsume Akatsuki, Kurone Mishima

**Rating:** 5/5

**Review Date:** 2026-02-13

---

## Review

This is a fun comedic story that expertly pokes fun at common tropes of the genre. The lighthearted approach and self-aware humor make it an entertaining read that doesn't take itself too seriously, delivering laughs while still providing an engaging narrative.

## What I Liked

- Funny and well-executed comedy throughout
- Main character is pretty reasonable and relatable
- Quick, easy pacing that keeps the story moving

## What I Disliked

- I know it's making fun of the tropes, but some of the characters still get annoying at times
- Contains some pervy elements
